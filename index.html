About Frosty SMP

Frostysmp, also written as FrostySMP, is a whitelisted minecraft serer started in August 2021 by VanshGamer. They have had a total of two vanilla worlds and they are currently in their second vanilla season. The server is currently administered by Darshi, who was one of the earliest member of server. The members of the Frosty servers are Minecraft YouTubers who have been recognised by other Frosty Member and have been invited to join.
